Welcome to Blackjack

Your objective is to be the dealers score. To do this you either need get 21 or as close to 21 with out going over. You and the dealer each start with 2 cards. From there you will "HIT"(be dealt more cards, 1 at a time). If your combined score is under 21("ACES" can be either 1 or 11) and face cards("JACK", "QUEEN" & "KING") are worth 10) and you no longer wish to "HIT" you would "STAY". When you stay play go to the dealer and he them must either "HIT" or "STAY". The dealer works a little differently though. The dealer must "HIT" as long as his hand is under 17, so keep that in mind when you are deciding your next move. There will be more tips to follow as you play. Good Luck!
